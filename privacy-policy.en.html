Privacy Policy

Product: HomeProof · Effective: June 2, 2026 · Last updated: June 2, 2026

HomeProof is a fully local tool for organizing household receipts, warranty cards, manuals, and warranty reminders. It is built privacy-first: all of your data stays on your device. No account is required, and nothing is uploaded to any server.

In short: HomeProof does not collect, store in the cloud, or transmit any of your personal data. Everything—including photos, receipts, manuals, serial numbers, and text-recognition results—is kept only on your iPhone or iPad.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information. HomeProof has no user accounts, requires no sign-up or login, and never sends your data to us or to any third party.

The content you create in the app—such as item names, brands, models, serial numbers, purchase dates, prices, stores, notes, and any photos, receipts, warranty cards, or manuals you import or scan—is entered by you and stored only on your device. The developer cannot access it.

2. How Device Permissions Are Used

HomeProof requests the following system permissions only when you use the related feature, and all processing happens on-device:

  • Camera: to scan receipts, warranty cards, manuals, and product nameplates. Captured content is saved only on your device and is never uploaded.
  • Photos: to import images you choose. We use the system photo picker, which gives the app access only to the photos you select—not your entire library.
  • Notifications: to deliver warranty-expiry reminders locally. Reminders are generated and scheduled entirely on-device; no remote push service is used.
  • Face ID / Touch ID (biometrics): for an optional privacy lock that protects locally stored data. Biometrics are handled by the iOS Secure Enclave; HomeProof never accesses, reads, or stores your biometric data.

You can change or revoke these permissions at any time in system Settings. Revoking a permission may disable the related feature but does not affect the safety of data you have already saved.

3. Text Recognition (OCR)

HomeProof uses Apple's on-device Vision framework for text recognition. The entire process runs locally on your device. Recognized text is never uploaded or shared. Results are offered only as suggestions for you to confirm; you decide what to save.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored in the app's local sandbox using the system database (Core Data) and file storage.
  • Attachment files use iOS File Protection and are protected by system encryption while the device is locked.
  • You can enable a privacy lock that requires Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode to open the app.
  • We rely solely on the encryption and security capabilities built into Apple's operating systems; we do not implement any custom cryptography.

5. Data Sharing and Selling

We do not sell, rent, or share any of your data. Because your data never leaves your device, we are technically unable to access or transfer it. HomeProof integrates no advertising, analytics, tracking, or social-sharing third-party SDKs.

6. Third-Party Components

The shipping version of HomeProof uses a single third-party open-source component, SnapKit, for user-interface layout. It performs no networking, collects no data, and contains no advertising or tracking. The app includes no other third-party data-collection components.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you remove it. You can delete individual items and their attachments within the app, or remove all local data at once by deleting the app. Deletion removes the data from your device, and we hold no backup that could restore it.

8. Children's Privacy

HomeProof is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children. Because the app collects no data, no data collection involving children takes place.

9. Your Rights

Under regulations such as the GDPR and the CCPA, you generally have rights to access, correct, delete, and opt out of the sale of your personal data. Because HomeProof does not collect or hold any of your personal data, that data stays entirely under your control on-device: you can view, edit, and delete it at any time within the app.

10. International Users

HomeProof is distributed globally. Because data is stored only on your device and is not transferred across borders, the app involves no international transfer of personal data.

11. Changes to This Policy

If a feature change affects our privacy practices, we will update this policy and revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Significant changes will be noted in the app's release notes.
